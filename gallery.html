GlassProtech Bus Window Protection

BUS WINDOW PROTECTION

Client Requirement:
To reduce the instances of intentional glass scratching on bus windows and to improve the internal environment for bus users.
Solution:
Printed anti-graffiti film was applied internally to the 4 rear windows which historically is the area most tagged. The imagery used was route maps and localised montage of Sydney landmarks.
  • Glass protection and increased security provided within the solution.
  • Glass panes protected from paint, pen or acid attack.
  • Reduced the risk of injury from breaking glass.
  • Film provided a higher level of UV protection to commuters.
  • Translucent film complied with security requirement to have unimpaired visibility.
  • Unimpaired visibility for the public on both sides of filmed panes.
  • Reduced maintenance cost of glass pane replacement.
  • Improved the ambience and commuter environment within the bus.

GlassProtech Commercial Retail Complex

ANTI- GRAFFITI SOLUTION - COMMERCIAL RETAIL COMPLEX

Client Requirement:
A new retail complex in an area prone to graffiti that had clear film installed on windows but was being heavily scratch tagged. The client was looking for a temporary cost effective solution that lessened the visual impact of existing scratch graffiti but still presented a good appearance to any prospective tenants.

  • Less costly that replacing the existing scratched anti-graffiti film.
  • Improved the chances of tenancy uptake of the premises.
  • Minimal light reduction internally.
  • Improved the ambience within the retail hub.

GlassProtech Clean Window Policy for Bus Fleet

CLEAN WINDOW POLICY FOR BUS FLEET

Client Requirement:
To comply with both company and local authority policy on clean scratch free windows there was a requirement for a proactive program on keeping windows filmed and scratch free.
Solution:
Used a built for purpose Anti-Graffiti film that lessened the impact of most existing etching and provided ongoing protection of windows. The film also comes off clean leaving no residue adhesive. Provided an effective, auditable and manageable maintenance program. Small numbered labels under each replaced film allowed the client to reconcile invoices and track frequency of replacements. An audit program was introduced to manage costs and fleet maintenance. Programmed access to fleet around timetables.
  • Glass protection, and increased security provided within the solution.
  • Glass panes protected from scratch, paint, pen or acid attack.
  • Reduced the risk of injury from breaking glass.
  • Film provided a higher level of UV protection to passengers.
  • Translucent film complied with security requirement to have unimpaired visibility.
  • Quicker removal of old film and no adhesive residue reduces maintenance cost
  • Reduced maintenance cost of window replacement.
  • Extended life span of scratched glass windows.
  • Translucent film complied with security requirement to have unimpaired visibility.
  • Improved the ambience and commuter environment within the bus.
